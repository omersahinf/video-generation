# Explainer Video Generator

Turn any prompt or document into an animated explainer video — no subscriptions, no cloud.

**Input:** Text prompt or document  
**Output:** MP4 video with voiceover, illustrations, and animations

**Styles:** 9+ visual styles (whiteboard, chalkboard, modern minimal, sharpie, playful, infographics, classic B&W, professional clean, and more)

**100% free stack:** Ollama (local LLM) + edge-tts (free TTS) + HuggingFace free tier
